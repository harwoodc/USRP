Itsy Bitsy

The Itsy-Bitsy Racers training group is for children who can swim but need more instruction before joining the Little Racers. ​​​
​Annual Fee: $25
Monthly fees: $35 for two 30 minute sessions per week.
Practice times: Seasonal and varies

Payment for ITSY's

Little Racers

This group includes young and beginning swimmers generally between the ages of 5 to 11 with minimal swim experience but who are able to swim one length of the pool unaided. The Little Racers’ instruction focuses on developing solid fundamentals necessary for success in competitive swimming: proper stroke technique, breathing, swim terminology, using a pace clock, plus gaining strength through kicking. Having fun and making friends is vital at this stage ​​​
New swimmers: $115 in
Renewals: $85
Monthly fees: $60 for three 45 minute sessions per week.
